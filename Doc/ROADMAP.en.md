# Project Roadmap

This document outlines planned features, in-progress tasks, and the long-term vision of the **Treasure Hunters** project.

## 🚀 Planned Features
- **Audio System**: Adding background music (BGM) and sound effects (SFX) for jumping, sword swinging, and coin collection.
- **New Stages**: 
  - Underwater Level with swimming mechanics.
  - Dark Forest Stage with dynamic lighting.
- **Boss System**: A fight against the pirate captain at the end of the game.
- **Checkpoint System**: Allowing players to respawn mid-stage instead of starting over upon defeat.

## 🚧 In Progress
- **Visual Enhancements**: Adjusting wider platforms for enemies and coins.
- **Decoration Optimization**: Adding animated palm trees and other environmental props.
- **Bug Fixes**: Adjusting collision hitboxes on tree trunks and fixing cloud background positioning.

## 💡 Future Ideas
- **Leaderboard**: Integrating a simple backend (like Firebase or Supabase) to save best times and high scores.
- **Additional Characters**: Adding alternative playable characters.
- **Mobile Support**: Implementing touch controls (virtual d-pad and action buttons) so the game can be played on mobile browsers.

## 🔬 Research Topics
- How to implement Tilemaps (via Tiled) instead of hardcoding terrain blocks in `main.js`.
- Utilizing Phaser's Particle Engine for cannon explosion effects and water splashes.
